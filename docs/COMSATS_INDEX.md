## What's Included

### Problems Addressed
1. Lack of Quality Applicants
2. Lack of Consistency in Startups
3. Poor Market Understanding by Startups
4. Absence of Proper Startup Lifecycle @ SSBC/BIC
5. Absence of Niche Incubators/Mentors
6. Lack of Faculty Role in Preparing Entrepreneurs
7. Lack of Networking & Funding Opportunities
8. Lack of Linkage Between Academia and Industry
